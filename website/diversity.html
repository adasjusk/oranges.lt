Diversity Statement

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Our Commitment

At oranges.lt, we believe that the best communities and the best software are built when everyone has a seat at the table. We are committed to fostering a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all people, regardless of who they are or where they come from.

Who We Welcome

We welcome and support people of all:

  • Races, ethnicities, and national origins
  • Gender identities and expressions
  • Sexual orientations
  • Ages
  • Religions and beliefs (or lack thereof)
  • Disabilities, whether visible or invisible
  • Socioeconomic backgrounds
  • Experience levels — from beginners to experts
  • Languages spoken

Our Community Standards

In all oranges.lt spaces — including our game servers, Discord, GitHub repositories, and this website — we expect all community members to:

  • Be respectful: Treat everyone with dignity and kindness. Disagreements happen, but they should never turn into personal attacks.
  • Be inclusive: Use welcoming language. Avoid slurs, derogatory terms, and exclusionary jokes.
  • Be constructive: Offer helpful feedback and support. We all learn by working together.
  • Be accountable: If you make a mistake, own it, learn from it, and move forward.

What We Don't Tolerate

The following behaviors are not acceptable in any oranges.lt space:

  • Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
  • Hate speech, slurs, or discriminatory language
  • Doxxing or sharing others' private information without consent
  • Sexual harassment or unwelcome sexual attention
  • Deliberate exclusion or gatekeeping based on identity
  • Trolling intended to harass or demean others

Enforcement

We take violations of these standards seriously. Depending on the severity, consequences may include:

  • A private warning from a moderator or administrator
  • Temporary mute or ban from game servers or Discord
  • Permanent ban from all oranges.lt services

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this statement, please report it to us at software@oranges.lt or contact a moderator on our Discord server.

Open Source and Open Doors

As an open-source organization, openness is at our core. We believe that diverse perspectives lead to better code, better games, and better communities. Whether you're a seasoned developer or someone who just wants to play on our Minecraft server, you belong here.
