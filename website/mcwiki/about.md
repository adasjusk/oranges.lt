Build, destroy, raid, and explore a vast, ungoverned landscape where your imagination and survival skills are the only boundaries.

# Commands for Orange MC

- /stuck - unstucks you from stucky places
- /autosort - sorts items in the chest similar to terraria
- /msg playername - you pm that player without others seeing
- /login passwd passwd - your password two times
- /register passwd passwd - you have to write this after captcha
- /home nameofhome - tps you to your beloved home/base
- /escape - same as stuck command but other prefix
- /delhome nameofhome - deletes teleportation to that place
- /sethome nameofhome - sets your home for teleportation
- /captcha - confirms that you are not robot
- /changepassword - type your old password then new two times
