# Server Plugin List

**LateSpawn**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/adasjusk/LateSpawn)
- **Description:** Spawns players who join late to the round.

**RemoteKeycard**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/Unbistrackted/RemoteKeycard)
- **Description:** Use keycards without having them in your hand all the time.

**Talky**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/tayjay/Talky)
- **Description:** Adds facial animations while talking.

**Scanner (Announcer)**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/adasjusk/Scanner)
- **Description:** Scans who is in the facility and announces it via C.A.S.S.I.E announcement. 

**IntercomDisplayInfo**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/arannnn7808/IntercomDisplayInfo)
- **Description:** Turns the Intercom into a live stats HUD. Displays round timer, team counts, and Intercom status.

**RespawnTimer**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/MedveMarci/RespawnTimer)
- **Description:** Shows when the next respawn wave will happen. 

**DiscordOnline**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/adasjusk/DiscordO)
- **Description:** Adds Online or Offline state to Discord bot.

**AntiStrangulation**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/ui2506/AntiStrangulation)
- **Description:** Plugin to configure SCP-3114.

**ExplodingPeanut**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/FrikandelbroodjeCommunity/ExplodingPeanut)
- **Description:** When SCP-173 dies, they explode. The chance and timing are configurable.

**SCP079RadioHack**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/SareanAka/SCP079RadioHack-LabAPI)
- **Description:** Allows SCP-079 to hack into the NTF radio.

**SCP500FunnyPlugin**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/adasjusk/FunnyPils)
- **Description:** Redesigned SCP-500 that gives funny effects similar to Chaos Theory server plugin.

**ThanosCoinPlugin**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/Pogromca-SCP/ThanosCoinPlugin)
- **Description:** Perfectly balanced plugin. Every coin flip has 50% chance to kill the player. Are you ready to test your luck?

**RgdCleanup**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/adasjusk/RgdCleanup)
- **Description:** Ragdoll and debris cleaner.

**ProjectMER**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/Michal78900/ProjectMER)
- **Description:** Plugin allowing to spawn and modify various objects.

**CedMod**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/CedModV2/CedMod)
- **Description:** Enhances the moderation experience by providing utilities that assist in a moderator's efforts.

**IOPluginRestart**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/adasjusk/IOPRestart)
- **Description:** Forces the server to restart (IOProccessRestart / InternalOpenPluginRestart).

**AutoTFF**
- **Link:** [GitHub](https://github.com/adasjusk/AutoTFF)
- **Description:** Automatically turns Friendly Fire on/off. Turns on when round ends and off when round starts.
